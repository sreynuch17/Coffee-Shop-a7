In general, when referring to "aromatic" coffee, it usually means coffee that has a distinct and pleasant smell. Coffee beans naturally release a variety of aromatic compounds during the roasting process, which contribute to the overall aroma and flavor of the brewed coffee.

Iced milk coffee

Iced coffee

Iced coffee is a popular cold beverage made by brewing coffee and serving it chilled over ice. It provides a refreshing and invigorating alternative to hot coffee, particularly during hot weather or when a cooler drink is desired.

Turkish Coffee

Turkish coffee is a traditional method of preparing and serving coffee that originated in the Middle East and is widely enjoyed in Turkey and other parts of the region. It is known for its strong flavor, rich texture, and unique brewing process.

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee that is brewed by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely ground coffee beans. It is known for its strong flavor, rich body, and the layer of foam on top called "crema."
