Tea Menu

PASSION MILK

"Passion milk" is not a standard or widely recognized term or beverage. It is possible that "passion milk" refers to a concoction or recipe that combines passion fruit flavor with milk.

THAI TEA FROM SCRATCH

This sweet and creamy Thai Tea recipe from scratch is a decadent iced tea using real Thai spices, rich sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and real tea.

BROWN SUGAR BUBBLE TEA

Brown sugar bubble tea, also known as brown sugar milk tea with boba, is a popular variation of bubble tea that features a rich and caramel-like flavor. It typically consists of black tea, brown sugar syrup, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls (boba).

LEMON RED TEA

Lemon red tea, also known as red tea with lemon, is a refreshing beverage made by combining red tea (often referred to as rooibos tea) with the tangy flavor of lemon.

TARO MILK TEA

Taro milk tea is a popular beverage that combines the earthy flavor of taro root with creamy milk and tea.

STRAWBERRY MILK BUBBLE TEA

Strawberry milk bubble tea is a delightful and refreshing beverage that combines the fruity flavor of strawberries, creamy milk, and chewy tapioca pearls (commonly known as boba).

BUTTETFLY PEA FLOWER TEA

Butterfly Pea Flower Tea is a unique and visually striking herbal tea made from the flowers of the Clitoria ternatea plant, also known as butterfly pea. The tea is known for its vibrant blue color, which can change to purple when lemon or lime juice is added.

BUTTERFLY PEA MILK TEA

Butterfly pea milk tea is a variation of the popular butterfly pea flower tea that incorporates the creaminess of milk. It combines the vibrant blue color of butterfly pea flower infusion with the richness of milk, resulting in a visually stunning and flavorful beverage.
